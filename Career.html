Potential Options

When I think about what I want to do when I grow up I have two main ideas. One is to join the army and become a high ranking officer. Two is to become some form of freelance programmer or database manager.

Army

My first choice when it comes to career opportunities is the army because it is a very structured work environment with high job security and with very nice benefits.

Freelance Programmer

My second choice when it comes to career opportunities is becoming a freelance programmer because of the total freedom and the potential to earn vast sums of money.
